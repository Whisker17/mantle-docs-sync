title: "Connecting Wallet to Mantle Network"

You can connect your wallet to the Mantle Network in different ways, here we present you the following two ways:

## Add by Using Chainlist[​](https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/users/how-to/wallet#add-by-using-chainlist) <a href="#add-by-using-chainlist" id="add-by-using-chainlist"></a>

You can automatically add Mantle Network to your wallet with one click using **Chainlist** by navigating to the following pages:

* [Add Mantle v2 Tectonic Mainnet](https://chainlist.org/chain/5000)
* [Add Mantle V2 Tectonic Sepolia Testnet](https://chainlist.org/chain/5003)

Simply connect your wallet to the web page, click on the '**Add to Metamask**' button that shows up, and approve the action in your wallet.

## Add Network Manually[​](https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/users/how-to/wallet#add-network-manually) <a href="#add-network-manually" id="add-network-manually"></a>



#### Step


### Launch Metamask

Launch [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/download/) and login to your wallet.



#### Step


### Add Network

The default network setting for the wallet is generally '**Ethereum Mainnet**'. Click on the button in the top-left to bring up a dropdown menu. Click on '**Add Network**' at the bottom of the dropdown menu.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-1-4c343f717ee5c76ceb3174cff4ff2daf.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Choose Manually

This will take you the '**Add a network**' page where you'll see multiple popular networks. Click on '**Add a network manually**' to bring up a form

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-2-58c012d71feff3ce45912a680768ec70.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Configure Network Infomation

Fill in the following info and click '**Save**' to finish. The network option will appear in the network setting dropdown menu once you're done.

## Add $MNT to Your Wallet[​](https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/users/how-to/wallet#add-mnt-to-your-wallet) <a href="#add-mnt-to-your-wallet" id="add-mnt-to-your-wallet"></a>

### Prepare To Import Tokens

Navigate to your wallet, switch to the **Sepolia Testnet** and click on the '**Import tokens**' button at the bottom to bring up a form.

### Enter the Token Address

Enter the `$MNT` token contract address `0x65e37B558F64E2Be5768DB46DF22F93d85741A9E` , and as you do that the other two fields will get populated automatically.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/add-custom-token-73776306b75c79729773510acad6d39a.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Finish Importing

Next, click on the '**Next**' button at the bottom, and then click '**Import**' in the next window to finish. You should be able to see your current balance.



## Configure Your wallet[​](https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/users/how-to/wallet#configure-your-wallet) <a href="#configure-your-wallet" id="configure-your-wallet"></a>

Mantle v2 Tectonic has the ultimate user experience by optimizing the fee mechanism. Users can set up `basefee` and `priorityfee` in MetaMask to greatly reduce the fee for transfers. Here is a detailed guide on how to configure your wallet.



#### Step


### Create a Transaction

When you are ready to send a transaction using MetaMask, construct the transaction first, at which point we can see that the `Estimated fee` before it is set is a higher value.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-5-307e0865a654cff6b32d0e0cbdf1f048.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Prepare Your Transaction Configuration

Click the `Next` button, and then click the red box to enter the setting interface.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-6-c23111cc33bb16d7b70595aa341f73b7.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Configure the Gas Fee

Select the `Advanced` button and we can see that we can set the `Max base fee` and `Priority Fee`, see [here](/system-information/fee-mechanism/eip-1559-support#application-of-eip-1559-in-mantle-v2-tectonic) for the meaning of these two items. Here we set them to the lowest values, i.e. `Max base fee` is 0.02 gwei and `Priority Fee` is 0. Check the box below "Save these values as my default for the Mantle sepolia network. ", and then click the ❌ button in the upper right corner.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-7-fa653c4a460d25d11d9fca15501c1452.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



#### Step


### Confirm thr Transaction

Once the settings are complete, when you reconstruct the transaction, you will see that the `Estimated fee` will be very low, click `Confirm` to send the transaction.

<img src="https://docs-v2.mantle.xyz/assets/images/metamask-8-c9fe29570f11a7151d1c723fb1a90919.png" alt="" data-size="original" />



With this setup, you will enjoy extreamely high speed and low cost transaction processing in Mantle v2 Tectonic.
